Our Mission

Srotar Ashor is a non-profit organization based in Melbourne, Australia, with the express aim of nurturing and showcasing Bengali music and culture.

We believe in the power of music to unite communities, preserve cultural heritage, and create lasting memories. Through our programs, we provide a platform for Bengali artists to share their talents and for music enthusiasts to experience the rich traditions of Bangladesh.

Our Story

Srotar Ashor was formed when a few like-minded Bengalis were drawn together by their love of music and perhaps, from a shared sense of nostalgia about their country of origin, Bangladesh. This small group would meet at regular intervals to sing together, enjoy each other's company, and for that brief moment replicate their cultural life back home.

It soon became apparent that there were many in the community who were keen music enthusiasts, as well as many talented musicians, but lacked a forum to come together in a happy union. A conscious decision was thus made to include the wider audience and, as a result, Srotar Ashor was incorporated in December 2004 with the aim of organizing regular musical programs.

Each year Srotar Ashor organizes 2 theme-based programs drawing on the talents of its core members, as well as members of the community in Melbourne and other states in Australia. Since its inception, Srotar Ashor has staged well over 50 programs spanning across many genres – from the esoteric Indian classical music to the immensely popular band music.

Our Journey

2004
Srotar Ashor was officially incorporated in December with the mission to organize regular musical programs for the Bengali community in Melbourne.
2011
Launched our signature program "Koler Gaan" - marking the beginning of our documented cultural journey and community engagement.
2012-2016
Expanded our reach with diverse programs including "Gane Gane Char Doshok," "Bauliana," "Iti Srotar Ashor," and "Muhurto Gulo," establishing ourselves as a cornerstone of Bengali culture in Melbourne.
Present
Continuing our legacy with innovative programs while maintaining our commitment to free entry and community accessibility, supported by member contributions and the Victorian Multicultural Commission.

Community Impact

Over the years, Srotar Ashor has created a lot of goodwill in the community through its commitment to excellence and quality. This was only possible because of the untiring and selfless effort of its members. The membership is an eclectic blend of youthful exuberance and middle-aged contemplation united by one common goal.

Entrance to all our programs is free. Programs are funded by contributions from members, generous music enthusiasts, and the Victorian Multicultural Commission. This ensures that Bengali music and culture remain accessible to everyone in our community.

The members are committed to arranging many more programs in the coming days and enjoy every step of the way. Our programs span many genres – from esoteric Indian classical music to immensely popular band music, ensuring there's something for every musical taste.

Connect With Us

We welcome music enthusiasts, artists, and community members to join us in our mission to preserve and celebrate Bengali culture. Whether you're a performer looking for a platform or a music lover seeking cultural connection, Srotar Ashor offers a warm and inclusive community.
